SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar 2022–2023

This is the webpage of the SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar. We meet every Monday at 16:00 in Room 136. Talks are 90 minutes long. The topic for the Spring semester is Geometric invariant theory, we will use Newstead's Introduction to Moduli Problems and Orbit Spaces.

Detailed schedule

  • Monday, 17 April 2023, Elisa Vitale (SISSA): Introduction to quotients in algebraic geometry.
  • Monday, 8 May 2023, Ajay Gautam (SISSA): Tanaka's theorem and affine quotients.
  • Monday, 15 May 2023, Matteo Montagnani (SISSA): Projective quotients.
  • Monday, 29 May 2023, Sohaib Khalid (SISSA): Mumford's criterion for GIT stability.
